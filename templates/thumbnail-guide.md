# Thumbnail Checklist & Title Formulas

## Thumbnail Checklist
Before publishing, verify EVERY thumbnail hits these marks:

### Visual Impact
- [ ] **Clear focal point** — One main element, not cluttered
- [ ] **High contrast** — Subject pops from background
- [ ] **Faces work** — Human face with emotion (curiosity, shock, excitement)
- [ ] **Text readable** — Large, bold, max 3-4 words
- [ ] **Mobile-friendly** — Looks good at small size

### Psychology Hooks
- [ ] **Pattern interrupt** — Unexpected element that stops the scroll
- [ ] **Curiosity gap** — Makes them NEED to know more
- [ ] **Clear promise** — They know what they'll get
- [ ] **Urgency or stakes** — Why watch NOW?

### Technical
- [ ] 1280x720 minimum resolution
- [ ] Under 2MB file size
- [ ] Tested in both light and dark mode

## Title Formulas (Finance/Trading Niche)

### The "I Wish I Knew" Formula
**Template:** "I wish I knew [specific technique] before [painful outcome]"
- "I wish I knew about options theta before losing $5K"
- "I wish I knew this tax hack before April"

### The "Stop Doing X" Formula
**Template:** "Stop [common mistake] — do this instead"
- "Stop checking your portfolio daily — do this instead"
- "Stop using Robinhood — here's what pros use"

### The "X Things" Formula
**Template:** "[Number] [adjective] signs you're [undesirable state]"
- "5 subtle signs you're bad with money"
- "3 red flags your broker doesn't want you to see"

### The "How I X" Formula
**Template:** "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe] (step-by-step)"
- "How I built a 6-figure dividend portfolio in 2 years"
- "How I pay $0 in capital gains tax (legally)"

### The "Most People Don't" Formula
**Template:** "Most people don't know [surprising fact] about [topic]"
- "Most people don't know Roth IRAs have income limits"
- "Most people don't know about this free investing tool"

### The "One Thing" Formula
**Template:** "The ONE thing every [target] needs to know about [topic]"
- "The ONE thing every trader needs to know about wash sales"
- "The ONE thing every investor needs to know about inflation"

## Thumbnail + Title Combinations That Work

| Thumbnail | Title Style | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
